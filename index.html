About

A writer who
has lived it.

I'm Sumiya — a writer and marketer from Pakistan, now based in Bournemouth, England. I started my first business at 20, running a content operation in Karachi that employed women in remote roles. I made my first million PKR before I turned 21.

Then I moved to the UK to study. I worked in a fish and chips shop. I bartended. I delivered pizzas. I graduated with a Masters in Marketing Management from Bournemouth University. Four days before my ceremony, I landed a role at a UK EdTech company managing HubSpot, lifecycle campaigns, and CRM strategy across four brands.

I write the way I have lived — honestly, with no neat bow tied at the end. That is what makes content people actually read.
